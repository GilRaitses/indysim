Studies

ORIGINAL STUDY

An Analytic Hazard Kernel for Optogenetically-Driven Larval Reorientation

Population-level modeling of sensorimotor dynamics using a gamma-difference kernel with two timescales. Validated across 14 experiments and 701 larval tracks.

Key Findings

Original Study

τ₁ ≈ 0.3s — Fast excitatory timescale capturing initial sensory response

τ₂ ≈ 4s — Slow suppressive timescale underlying habituation

R² = 0.968 — Gamma-difference kernel fit quality

Follow-Up Study

25 events/track — Current data vs 100 required for phenotyping

8.6% — Fraction of tracks showing genuine individual variation

10× — Information gain from burst vs continuous protocol
